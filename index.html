Why Greenery Is Important?

Greenery plays a strategic role in making the world more feasible. Plants provide essential biodiversity, ecosystem services, carbon sequestration and helps to reduce the Urban Heat Island effect. They cool the air, provide oxygen, remove harmful gases and air pollutants, and keep the air moist. It is very important to preserve the existing greenery, we need to grow a huge amount of trees and plants for the existence of human beings on this planet.

Benefits of Greenery

Nowadays, we can say that the main part of the world population is living in cities, and greenery is used as a landmark in urban areas… Most of the cities are designed in such a way that most of the city is beautified with trees and plants which makes it look embellish. Greenery has a great effect on physical and mental health as well as it has a great effect on eyesight. Greenery helps to fight against pollution. A walk in the greenery has the power to alleviate your stress and to relax. Walking for one hour in greenery provides you with fresh oxygen which is a basic need of living things. Breathing pure oxygen helps you to…

  • energy levels.
  • improve mood.
  • concentration.
  • improve sports performance.
  • reduce stress.
  • provide relief for headaches and migraines.
  • promote better sleep.
